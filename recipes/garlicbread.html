Garlic Bread

Description

Homemade garlic bread

Ingredients

  • Italian bread
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 1/8 tsp onion salt
  • 1/4 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tbs grated parmesan

Steps

  1. Microwave butter to soften
  2. Add garlic, onion salt, and basil to butter. Stir to combine
  3. Spread button mixture on bread
  4. Sprinkle parmesan over the top and gently press down
  5. Broil low on baking sheet for 2-4min or until toasted
