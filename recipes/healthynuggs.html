Healthy Chicken Nuggets

Description

Healthier than usual nuggets

Ingredients

  • 2 large chicken breasts cubed
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt/pepper
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 6 tbs Italian breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbs panko
  • 2 tbs grated parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil spray

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425. Spray baking sheet with olive oil spray
  2. Put olive oil in one bowl. Mix breadcrumbs, panko, and parmesan in another bowl
  3. Season chicken with salt/pepper then coat in olive oil
  4. Put chicken in the breadcrumb mixture to coat then add to baking sheet
  5. Lightly spray the top with olive oil spray then bake for 8-10min. Turn over and cook another 4-5min or until cooked through
