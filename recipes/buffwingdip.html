Buffalo Chicken Wing Dip

Description

Spicy cheese dip with chicken and Buffalo hot sauce

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast, cooked and shredded
  • 1 cup Cheese, mixed and white cheddar
  • 1/4 cup ranch dressing
  • 1/2 cup buffalong wing saunce
  • 8oz cream cheese

Steps

  1. Bake Chicken Breast and shred
  2. While chicken is baking, mix all the other Ingredients in a baking dish
  3. When the chicken is done, shread and mix with the other ingredients
  4. Flatten the top of the mixture and cover with more cheese
  5. Bake in the oven for about 10 min
